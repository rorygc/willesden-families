Hi [Camp Name] team,

I'm a local parent — we used your [camps / holiday clubs] last summer and found them on the New Parent Company guide. I'm getting in touch to ask if you're running similar camps or activities this summer.

If so, could you please let me know:
- what ages you accept
- dates and times
- costs
- how to book
- any flyer or web link

Thanks very much!

Best regards,
Rory
