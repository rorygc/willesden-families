About this guide

Willesden Families started as a local parent resource and is now being expanded into a practical, searchable neighbourhood guide.

It includes childcare, cafés, indoor ideas, SEND support, weekly picks, shortlist tools, and a map view — with regular web searches to keep the content fresh.

Always double-check opening times, booking requirements, and safeguarding details with the venue or provider before you go.
