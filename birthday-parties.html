Jump In! Soft Play — party package

0–10££Cricklewood

Dedicated party rooms, inclusive soft-play access, food options (hot or cold), and a dedicated party host. Packages from £13.95 per child. The classic "turn up and let someone else handle it" option. Book 4–6 weeks ahead for weekends.

Why parents like it: Zero setup, zero cleanup, and kids are exhausted by the end. Party hosts actually run the activities.

Queen's Park

Queen's Park — outdoor party

all agesFreeQueen's Park

The perfect summer birthday venue: huge playground, duck pond, wide open lawns for games and picnics, and the Pavilion Café for hot drinks and ice creams. No booking needed for informal gatherings — just arrive early to claim a good spot. For proper parties, the park has a dedicated events area you can book through the City of London Corporation.

Why parents like it: Free, flexible, and kids genuinely love it. Bring a picnic blanket, balloons, and cake — done.

West Hampstead

The Ark Soft Play — party packages

0–8££West Hampstead

Cleaner, calmer soft-play parties with exclusive-use options. Packages include party food, a dedicated host, and full access to the play frame. Maximum 20 children. Particularly good for younger children's parties (1–4) where a smaller, less chaotic venue is ideal.

Why parents like it: Smaller and less overwhelming than the big chains — perfect for nervous or younger party-goers.

Swiss Cottage

Swiss Cottage Leisure Centre — pool party

5–12££Swiss Cottage

Swimming pool party with inflatables, floats, and games in the leisure pool. Packages include exclusive pool time with qualified lifeguards, a party room for food (bring your own or order from their café), and changing facilities. Maximum 20 swimmers per party. About 15 mins on the Jubilee line.

Why parents like it: Swimming parties are the ultimate "kids have a blast, no mess at home" option. Lifeguards included.

Everyman Cinema — private screening party

4–12£££Maida Vale

Hire a private screen at Everyman for a kids' birthday film screening. Sofa seating, blankets, popcorn and snacks included. You choose the film from their family catalogue. Packages for up to 30 children. The "wow factor" option that makes you the coolest parent in the class.

Why parents like it: Rain-proof, stress-proof, and genuinely special. Kids talk about these parties for months.

Chelsea

Clip 'n Climb — climbing party

4+£££Chelsea

Birthday climbing sessions with auto-belay walls, fun challenges, and an instructor-led session. Party room included for food and cake. All equipment provided. Maximum 15 climbers. About 30 minutes drive or accessible by Overground + bus. Worth the trip for the novelty factor.

Affordable community space with a garden, kitchen facilities, and indoor room. Perfect for a DIY party — you bring food, decorations, and entertainment. The garden is brilliant for summer parties. Book directly through Our Yard. Really reasonable rates compared to commercial venues.

Willesden Green

Little Kickers — football party

2–7££Willesden Green

Football-themed birthday party led by Little Kickers coaches. Games, mini-matches, skill challenges, and a medal for every child. They bring all the equipment — you just bring the kids and the cake. Indoor venues in winter, outdoor in summer. Up to 20 children.

Why parents like it: Coaches handle everything — games, crowd control, and keeping the birthday child feeling special. Medals included.

Willesden Green

Willesden Green Library — room hire

all ages£Willesden Green

Modern community rooms available for hire in the library building. Bright, accessible, with kitchen facilities and plenty of space. Particularly good for craft-party themes or anything that needs tables and chairs. Very affordable — check Brent Council website for current rates.

Why parents like it: Central Willesden Green location, excellent facilities, and genuinely reasonable pricing for a council venue.

Willesden Sports Centre — hall hire

4+£Willesden

Hire the sports hall for a bouncy castle, games, and running-around space. The classic "hall with a bouncy castle" party that never goes out of style. Kitchen facilities available. You bring everything else. Book through Better UK — council rates mean it's very affordable.

Paddington Recreation Ground — pavilion hire

all ages£Maida Vale

Hire the pavilion at one of London's best parks. Large green spaces, playground, café on site, and a proper indoor space with kitchen for food and cake. Outdoor games area included. Great for summer parties where you want both indoor shelter and outdoor play space.

Why parents like it: Best of both worlds — indoor backup if it rains, massive outdoor space if it's sunny. Café for parents to grab a coffee.
