# School summer activities outreach tracker

## Status legend
- **Queued** = email drafted, not yet sent
- **Sent** = email sent, waiting for reply
- **Replied** = school responded
- **Follow-up** = reminder needed

Subject: Summer camps / holiday activities at [School Name]?

Hi [School Name] team,

I’m getting in touch as a local parent to ask whether you’re running any summer camps, holiday clubs, or other activities for children over the summer.

If so, could you please share:
- the ages you accept
- dates and times
- costs
- how to book
- any flyer or web link you can send

Thanks very much — and if this isn’t something you offer, no problem.

Best regards,
Rory
